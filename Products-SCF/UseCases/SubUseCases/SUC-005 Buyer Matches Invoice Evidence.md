# SUC-005: Buyer Matches Invoice Evidence

Parent use case: UC-004  
Target sprint: Sprint 1.2  
Priority: P0

## Objective

Enable buyer to confirm whether invoice data aligns with source evidence.

## Steps

1. Buyer opens invoice review.
2. Platform displays invoice, PO reference, delivery reference, contract terms, tax, discounts, and beneficiary.
3. Buyer marks checks as passed or failed.
4. Platform records matching result.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Buyer can validate core evidence.
- Matching result is stored.
- Failed matching can lead to dispute, rejection, or hold.
