# SUC-018: Display Risk Mode and Recourse Terms

Parent use case: UC-010  
Target sprint: Sprint 2.1 / Sprint 4.1  
Priority: P1

## Objective

Show users the risk allocation attached to each financeable invoice.

## Steps

1. Platform assigns default risk mode.
2. Platform displays supplier exposure and investor exposure.
3. Supplier confirms representation/warranty terms before factoring request.
4. Investor reviews risk terms before funding.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Every financeable invoice has risk mode.
- Supplier sees recourse obligations.
- Investor sees risk mode before funding.
